- Bovine Respiratory Disease
-- Breeds: Angus, Hereford, Charolais
-- Symptoms: Coughing, Sneezing, Nasal Discharge
-- Remedies: Antibiotics, Anti-inflammatory drugs; Vaccination

- Foot and Mouth Disease
-- Breeds: All breeds
-- Symptoms: Blister-like sores on mouth, hooves, and teats; Drooling, Lameness
-- Remedies: Vaccination, Quarantine, Strict biosecurity measures

- Mastitis
-- Breeds: All breeds
-- Symptoms: Swollen udder, Abnormal milk, Fever
-- Remedies: Antibiotics, Udder hygiene, Milking hygiene
